Chapter 3:

Embracing Creativity But life had more in store for me beyond the confines of the warehouse. In the bustling world of radio, I discovered a newfound passion for creativity and expression. The turning point came when I ventured into the realm of education, embarking on a journey of discovery as a Full-time Adult Learner at GMIT Galway Mayo Institution of Technology. Chapter 5: Captaining the Airwaves

In the bustling city of Leeds, UK, a new chapter awaited me as the Manager of Radio Station at South Leeds Radio. Here, amidst the whirlwind of live broadcasts and studio sessions, I found my voice as a leader, orchestrating the day-to-day operations with precision and passion.

Chapter 6: A Symphony of Success

As the story unfolded, I found myself orchestrating grand events and celebrations, including The Gathering for Gary in Brighton, UK. These gatherings, brimming with music and merriment, brought communities together and showcased the power of collaboration and creativity

Chapter 7: Continuing the Journey

Today, the journey continues as I embark on new adventures as a Course Provider for Radio/Podcasting Production at Chapel FM, Leeds. Here, amidst the hum of creativity and the buzz of learning, I continue to expand my horizons, embracing the ever-changing landscape of digital media and communication.
